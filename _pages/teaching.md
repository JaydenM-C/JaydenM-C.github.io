### Future teaching and supervision

Join me in semester 1, 2024, for LING3350 _Structure of English_, offered on-campus at Callaghan and online.

**Honours supervision**

I am currently available to supervise honours students in most/all areas of linguistics (if I can't help you, I'll direct you to someone who can). Get in touch via email if you're interested in topic ideas or to propose your own topic.

**PhD supervision**

I am currently available to supervise PhD students. I have no funded PhD positions on offer, but competitive scholarship opportunities exist at the University of Newcastle. Please get in touch via email to discuss funding possibilities and potential projects.

### 2023 Semester 2

**LING3350:** _Structure of English_

Course coordinator. University of Newcastle, Callaghan campus and online. [Course handbook](https://www.newcastle.edu.au/course/LING3350). A course in English syntax. Despite the English focus, this course equips students with the core concepts for analysing and describing the grammatical structures of human languages more generally. The course is designed to be quite practical. We get our hands dirty with plenty of exercise sets.

### 2023 Semester 1

**LING3002:** _Syntax_

Course coordinator. University of Newcastle, Callaghan campus. [Course handbook](https://www.newcastle.edu.au/course/LING3002). Advanced undergraduate level course on syntactic theory, including minimalism. The course takes an engaging, data-driven approach, exploring data from languages of Australia and the Pacific, as well as English and other European languages.
